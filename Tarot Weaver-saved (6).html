Heal and Break

This 3-card spread is designed to illuminate pathways towards recovery and the necessary letting go, offering profound insights into your current situation and future steps.

Divination Ritual
01

The Wound (What Needs Healing)

This position identifies the core area of your life or psyche that is currently calling for attention. It represents the "break" that hasn't quite fused back together. Whether it's a past trauma, a recent disappointment, or a lingering insecurity, this card brings the source of your discomfort into the light of awareness so the healing process can truly begin.

Internal Focus The Foundation
02

The Break (What to Release)

Healing often requires a clean break from habits, people, or mindsets that are keeping the wound open. This position reveals what you must consciously detach from to move forward. It asks: "What weight are you carrying that no longer serves your journey?" Breaking these ties isn't an end, but a necessary clearing of space for new growth.

Active Release The Catalyst
03

The Mending (The Path Forward)

Finally, we look toward the resolution. This card offers guidance on how to integrate your experiences and find a sense of wholeness once more. It represents the strength gained from the struggle and the specific actions or attitudes that will lead you toward a state of peace. This is the gold that fills the cracks, making the vessel stronger and more beautiful than before.

Integration The Outcome
