30-Day Feng Shui
Principles Challenge

"Welcome, seeker. Over the next thirty days, we will harmonize the seen and unseen forces within your home. This journey is more than interior design; it is a sacred alignment of your environment with your soul's highest path." — Your Feng Shui Mentor

01
15 Mins

Understanding Qi

Mapping the Energetic Flow

Walk through your home imagining being a stream of water, noting where the flow feels blocked, stagnant, or rushed. Draw a rough floor plan and mark these stagnant areas.

Safety: Walk slowly, watch steps to avoid tripping on rugs or corners.

Reflection Prompt

Which area felt most welcoming? Which felt heavy or difficult to pass through?

Discussion:

How does physical flow relate to your emotional state? Did you find unexpected blockages?

02
20 Mins

The Mouth of Qi

Clearing the Energetic Gateway

Clean the front door (inside and out), the porch, and the entryway. Intend to invite positive energy into your home as you scrub away the dust.

Safety: Use non-toxic cleaners, be cautious of slippery steps, and keep the doorway clear of trip hazards.

Reflection Prompt

How did the entrance energy shift once it was clean and clear?

Discussion:

What energy do you want to cross your threshold? Do objects near the door send mixed messages?

03
20 Mins

Decluttering as Exorcism

Releasing Energetic Attachments

Choose a small drawer or surface and remove everything. Hold each item, sense its energy. Discard or donate items that feel draining or heavy.

Safety: Use proper lifting techniques; be careful with sharp objects or broken glass.

Reflection Prompt

Did you feel guilt, relief, or neutrality when letting go of these items?

Discussion:

How does physical clutter mirror mental or spiritual clutter? Why do we hold onto negative items?

04
30 Mins

The Command Position

Establishing Energetic Sovereignty

Evaluate your bed, desk, and stove. Can you see the door without being directly in line with it? Plan to move one piece into the 'command position'.

Safety: Get a partner for heavy furniture; do not block fire exits or walkways.

Reflection Prompt

How does your sense of personal power change based on your visibility of the entrance?

Discussion:

In what life areas are you not in command? How does physical placement affect psychic defenses?

05
15 Mins

Yin and Yang

Balancing Light and Shadow

Analyze a room for Yin (dark, soft, quiet) and Yang (bright, hard, loud) balance. Add Yin to a hyper-active room or Yang to a stagnant room.

Safety: Ensure cords are tucked away; avoid overloading electrical outlets.

Reflection Prompt

Do you personally gravitate more towards Yin or Yang environments?

Discussion:

How can Yin/Yang imbalance disrupt spiritual practices? What are creative ways to introduce Yang to windowless rooms?

06
25 Mins

The Bagua Map

Divining Your Floor Plan

Draw a tick-tack-toe grid over your floor plan, aligning the bottom row with the front door wall. Identify which room sits in the center zone.

Safety: Standard awareness needed; avoid walking into furniture while measuring.

Reflection Prompt

Which physical area of the map needs the most immediate repair or attention?

Discussion:

How does a space having "energetic anatomy" change your view of your home? What if a Bagua section is missing?

07
10 Mins

Water Element

Enhancing Intuition and Flow

Introduce a water element (bowl of fresh water, mirror, or black object) to the North (Career) area. Set an intention for clear intuition.

Safety: Keep water containers securely placed, away from electrical cords or appliances.

Reflection Prompt

Where is energy flowing smoothly, and where is it frozen in your life?

Discussion:

How can excessive Water energy manifest negatively? What occult practices rely on water energy?

08
15 Mins

Wood Element

Anchoring Ancestral Roots

Place a healthy plant or wooden object in the East (Family) or Southeast (Wealth) area to stimulate vitality and upward growth.

Safety: Ensure plants are non-toxic if pets are present.

Reflection Prompt

How do your roots and family history support your current growth?

Discussion:

Difference between a living plant and wooden furniture? How does upward growth apply to studies?

09
15 Mins

Fire Element

Igniting Will and Visibility

Light a candle in the South (Fame/Reputation) area to activate passion, transformation, and visibility.

Safety: Never leave a burning candle unattended. Place on heat-proof surface away from drafts.

Reflection Prompt

Are you comfortable being seen and recognized for your true self?

Discussion:

How is fire used for transformation in occult traditions? How to temper Fire if feeling burnt out?

10
15 Mins

Earth Element

Grounding the Astral Body

  • Place a natural crystal or bowl of salt in the Center or Northeast.
  • Sit nearby for 5 minutes.
  • Visualize roots extending from your spine into the earth.
Safety: Wash hands after handling soil. Keep small stones away from children/pets.

Reflection Prompt

What physical actions ground you when your energy feels scattered?

Discussion:

Why is grounding necessary before energy work? How does crystal energy differ from raw earth?

The Journey Continues...

Days 11 through 30 involve deep work with specific Bagua areas and protective wards.

30
10 Mins

Setting Intentions

Awakening the Spirit of the Home

Stand in the center of your home (Tai Qi), close your eyes, place hands on heart, and speak a dedication aloud. Thank the house spirit and declare this a sacred space.

Safety: Be mindful of surroundings while eyes are closed to avoid losing balance.

Final Reflection

How has your relationship with your home evolved into a spiritual partnership?

Mastery Discussion:

Does a house possess its own spirit? How will you maintain energetic hygiene moving forward?
