Aegis of the Unseen

Category

Warding against evil

Incantation

"By the light that pierces shadow, and the silence that commands fear, I weave a shield of purity. Let no ill intent, malevolent gaze, nor wicked spirit cross this threshold. So mote it be, sealed by the arcane decree."

Effect

This potent warding spell creates an invisible, shimmering barrier around a designated area or individual. It repels minor spirits, deflects curses, and alerts the caster to any significant breach attempts by hostile entities. The barrier is strongest against creatures or magic originating from truly evil sources.
